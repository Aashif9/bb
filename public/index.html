Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Leading Light.

How do you verify mentors?

We verify all mentors through a rigorous process that includes checking their academic credentials, CGPA certificates, and employment details. We also conduct video interviews to ensure they are who they claim to be.

How much does it cost to access reviews?

We offer different subscription tiers starting at ₹499 per month for basic access to verified reviews. Premium tiers that include mentor sessions start at ₹999 per month.

Can I become a mentor if my CGPA is below 9.0?

Our platform primarily focuses on mentors with a CGPA of 9.0 or higher. However, we make exceptions for professionals with exceptional career achievements or those from specialized fields where such high CGPAs are uncommon.

How are mentor sessions conducted?

Mentor sessions are conducted through our secure in-platform video and chat system. You can schedule sessions based on the mentor's availability and your convenience. Each session typically lasts 30-45 minutes.

What if I'm not satisfied with a mentor session?

We offer a satisfaction guarantee for all mentor sessions. If you're not satisfied with a session, you can request a refund or a free session with another mentor within 24 hours of the completed session.
